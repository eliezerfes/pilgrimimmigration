The most complete solutions on the market.

Ten service packages covering the main paths to business and professional immigration to the United States. Explore each one to understand what is included, the execution timeline, and the investment range.

i.

EB-1A — Extraordinary Ability

Green card for professionals of extraordinary ability with national or international recognition in their field. Aimed at those at the top of their field who want to demonstrate outstanding achievements.

ii.

EB-1C — Executives and Managers

Green card for multinational executives and managers transferred to a company in the U.S. Aimed at leaders who will head an American operation tied to a group abroad.

iii.

EB-2 NIW — National Interest

Green card with a job offer waiver, for those proposing an endeavor of national interest to the U.S. Aimed at professionals and entrepreneurs with an impactful project.

iv.

EB-2 / EB-3 with Sponsor

Green card based on a job offer from a sponsoring American employer. Aimed at professionals who have (or are seeking) a sponsor willing to conduct the certification process.

v.

E-2 — Treaty Investor

Visa for investors from treaty countries who invest capital in an American business. Aimed at entrepreneurs who want to operate and grow a company in the United States.

vi.

L-1 — Intracompany Transfer

Visa to transfer executives, managers, or specialists from a company abroad to a U.S. subsidiary. Aimed at groups that are opening or expanding an American operation.

vii.

O-1 — Extraordinary Ability

Temporary visa for individuals of extraordinary ability in sciences, business, arts, education, or athletics. Aimed at outstanding talents who will work in the U.S. in their field.

viii.

H-1B — Specialty Occupation

Work visa for specialty occupations requiring a higher education degree. Aimed at professionals with a job offer from an American employer in a qualified position.

ix.

Business Plan

Complete, investor-ready business plan, structured chapter by chapter. Aimed at those who need the foundational document that supports the business proposal of the immigration case.

x.

Change of Status

Application to change immigration status for those already in the U.S. who need to move from one category to another. Aimed at those who want to adjust their situation without leaving the country.
